Origin

Started with one
overcoat, made twice.

Vantageflow began with a single overcoat pattern, cut and re-cut a dozen times until it fit right in motion, not just on a hanger. That obsession with how a garment actually behaves once it leaves the fitting room is still the whole business. We're a small studio — not a factory — and we'd rather sell out of eleven silhouettes than dilute into forty.

Every fabric is sourced from mills we visit in person. Every sample goes through a full year of real wear — hot season, rain, travel — before it's approved for production.

How We Work

A slower process,
on purpose.

STAGE 01

Sourcing

We work directly with four mills across West Africa and Europe, chosen for fiber quality and how they treat their labor, not just price per yard.

STAGE 02

Pattern & Fit

Every silhouette goes through a minimum of six fit sessions across different body types before a pattern is locked.

STAGE 03

Small-Batch Production

We produce in batches of under 200 units per colorway, which means fewer sizes run out — but also means some things won't come back.

STAGE 04

Fit Exchange

If a size is wrong, we cover the exchange shipping both ways within 7 days of delivery — no restocking fee, no questions about wear.

The Studio

Small team.
Strong opinions about fabric.

Vantageflow is run by a team of six based in Lagos, spanning pattern-making, sourcing, and fulfillment. We reply to every fit question ourselves.
